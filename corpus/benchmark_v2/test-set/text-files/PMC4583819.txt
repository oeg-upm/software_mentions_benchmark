
All antibodies were purchased from BD Biosciences/Pharmingen (Franklin Lakes, NJ). Cells were resuspended in FACS buffer (1% FBS/1% pooled human serum in phosphatebuffered saline) and stained with fluorochrome-conjugated antibodies for 20 minutes at 48C, washed 3 times, and analyzed on a Becton Dickinson (Franklin Lakes, NJ) FACSCaliber. Data were analyzed using FlowJo software (TreeStar, Ashland, OR).
