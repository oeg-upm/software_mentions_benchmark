
The vital sign extraction was performed using HL7 interface and Extract-Transfer-Load (ETL) tools in the Mount Sinai Data Warehouse (MSDW). The MSDW contains data extracted from twenty of Mount Sinai's clinical and financial systems, including all 2.7 million patients in the Master Person Index and over one billion facts for these patients. Database searches are performed with an internally developed Cohort Query Tool written in Java. The MSDW is compliant with HIPAA and New York State privacy and security regulations and with institutional policy regarding protection of human subjects and participation in research.
