
The between-groups difference in the proportion of participants making a change in behavior related to their osteoporosis risk was analyzed using the odds ratio and 95% confidence interval (CI). The between-group difference in knowledge change score was analyzed using an independent group t-test and 95% CI of the difference score. McNemar's chi-square was used to analyze the change from baseline to follow-up in the proportion of the treatment group with adequate vitamin D intake. Study outcomes were analyzed on a per protocol basis. All analyses were conducted using OpenEpi 3.03a (www.openepi.com).
