
To calculate the sample size, we used the software Epi-Info 6.04. We considered the population of 3,608 adolescents in the age range and sex for the study (16), the prevalence of excess body fat of 25% (17), an acceptable variability of 11% and a confidence level of 95%, with minimum sample of 59 adolescents in each group (G1 and G2), totaling a sample of 118 adolescents.
