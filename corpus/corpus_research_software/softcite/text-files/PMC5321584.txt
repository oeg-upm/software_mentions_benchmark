
Statistical analysis was performed with IBM SPSS Statistics v. 22.0 (SPSS Inc., Chicago, IL, USA). The mean and the standard deviation of each group were calculated. All data were analyzed with Mann-Whitney U test to check for significant differences among the groups. Wilcoxon signedrank tests were also used to analyze the effects of the direction of the dislodging force within the groups. All analyses were performed at 95% level of confidence (Î± = .05).
